SUCHITA MISTRY

Suchita Mistry is a sixteen year old incoming high-school senior who is interested in computer engineering and finance. Her dream is to open up her own tech company and build an app. Through Girls Who Code and a Stevens summer program, she has furthered her passion for coding and learned languages such as Python, C++, C, HTML, CSS, PHP and Javascript. Suchita has also won the President's Volunteer Service Award for her volunteer work with children. Some of her hobbies include dancing, reading, and watching Bollywood movies.

Suchita's friends best describe her as ambitious, resourceful, and witty. Something Suchita loves doing is hanging out with her friends. Her biggest achievement is building girl unity. A fun fact about Suchita is that she loves eggplant in her pasta, and is a vegetarian.

ELIZABETH SONG

Elizabeth Song is a fifteen year old incoming high-school junior who would like to major in business and computer science. Currently, her favorite TV show is "The 100". Elizabeth is a Girl Scout, who is currently working on her Gold Award, and participates in her school's Unicef, Future Business Leaders of America, and Robotics. With Girls Who Code, Elizabeth has increased her knowledge in languages such as PHP (MYSQL database), HTML, CSS, Java, Python, and Arduino C++.

Elizabeth's friends best describe her as business-oriented, considerate, and determined. She plays the violin as well, and started a Girl Up club in her school. Her biggest achievement is building communities all over the world through girl unity. A fun fact about Elizabeth is that her middle name has only two letters.
