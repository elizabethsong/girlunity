SUCHITA MISTRY

Suchita Mistry is a eighteen year old college freshman who is interested in computer science and finance. Her dream is to open up her own tech company and build an app. Through Girls Who Code and a Stevens summer program, she has furthered her passion for coding and learned languages such as Python, C++, C, HTML, CSS, PHP and Javascript. Suchita has also won the President's Volunteer Service Award for her volunteer work with children.

Suchita's friends best describe her as ambitious, resourceful, and witty. Something Suchita loves doing is hanging out with her friends. Her biggest achievement is building girl unity.

ELIZABETH SONG

Elizabeth Song is a sixteen year old high-school senior who would like to major in finance and minor in computer science. Elizabeth is a Girl Scout, who is currently working on her Gold Award by leading an art program at Eden Autism, and is the Varsity Business Captain of FIRST Robotics. With Girls Who Code, Elizabeth has increased her knowledge in languages such as PHP/phpMyAdmin (MySQL database), HTML, CSS, Java, Python, and Arduino C++.

Elizabeth's friends best describe her as business-oriented, considerate, and determined. She has co-started a Girl Up club in her school and her biggest achievement is building communities all over the world through Girl Unity.
